14 July

Every Birthday Matters. No One Should Celebrate Alone.

Our Mission

14 July is a free community where people from around the world come together to wish happy birthday to anyone who might otherwise celebrate alone. We believe that a simple birthday message can make someone feel seen, valued, and connected.

Whether you have a large family or no one to celebrate with, everyone deserves kindness on their special day.

How It Works

1. Create a free account.
2. Add your birthday to your profile.
3. On your birthday, members from around the world can send you birthday wishes.
4. Every member can send one free birthday wish each day to someone celebrating.

Why We Exist

Loneliness affects millions of people. A birthday should be a day of happiness, not sadness. Our goal is to build a global community where kindness brings people together, one birthday at a time.

Future Vision

As the community grows, we hope to partner with one sponsor each day. The sponsor can help support the platform while allowing the service to remain completely free for everyone.

Our Promise

- Free forever for users.
- A safe and welcoming community.
- Real birthday wishes from real people.
- Spreading kindness, one birthday at a time.

Because everyone deserves to hear: "Happy Birthday!"
